# field_reference.md

This quick reference lists common Bloomberg fields by category.

Pricing & Trading
- PX_LAST: Last price
- PX_VOLUME: Trading volume
- VOLUME_AVG_20D: 20-day average volume
- VOLATILITY_30D: 30-day volatility
- DAY_TO_DAY_TOT_RETURN_GROSS_DVDS: Daily total return (%)

Valuation & Fundamentals
- CUR_MKT_CAP: Market capitalization (millions)
- PE_RATIO: Price-to-earnings ratio
- PX_TO_BOOK_RATIO: Price-to-book ratio
- FREE_FLOAT_PCT: Free float percentage
- SALES_REV_TURN: Revenue
- EBITDA: EBITDA

Ownership & Corporate Actions
- TOP_20_HOLDERS_PUBLIC_FILINGS: Major shareholders (bulk)
- DVD_HIST_ALL: Dividend history (bulk)
- CAPITAL_CHANGE_DETAILS: Capital changes (bulk)

Identifier Formats
- Japan: {Code} JT Equity (e.g., 7203 JT Equity)
- US: {Ticker} US Equity (e.g., AAPL US Equity)
- Hong Kong: {Code} HK Equity (e.g., 700 HK Equity)
- Index: {Ticker} Index (e.g., NKY Index)
